iCalendar

iCalendar (often known simply as 'iCal') is a standard calendar format that enables users to create and share electronic calendars and scheduling information such as events, feast days, etc. The standard is supported by most modern e-mail and calendaring software such as Microsoft Outlook, Apple iCal, Google Calendar, Evolution, and Thunderbird. Providing the feasts in this format allows you to add them to your calendar on your computer or smart phone — and thus carry them with you every day.
